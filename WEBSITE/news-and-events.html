Recent News

Learn To Skate has started at Bay Skate

Learn to Skate session are now being offered at Hawke's Bay's premier skate park: Bay Skate


At 10am on Sunday's, you can drop by Bay Skate to enjoy the Roller Disco vibes, or to get assistive tutoring from skilled skaters


To learn more, visit the Bay Skate website or view their social media: Facebook and Instagram
