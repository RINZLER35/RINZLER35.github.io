Home

Hello, and welcome to the online home of the Action Sports community in Hawkes Bay, New Zealand.

We have a list of skateparks to visit, local clubs to get involved with, skate shops to get you sorted, and everything else you need to get you situated into the world of Action Sports in the bay.


Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a platform of current, relevant, and useful information regarding Action Sports in Hawkes Bay.


Goal

The goal of this website is to support and assist growth, activity, and development of the community of Action Sports in Hawkes Bay. Key performance indicators of this goal can be defined as:

  • High and/or regular activity at events, clubs, and parks
  • Good financial well-being of related events and commercial businesses
  • Good involvement from youth
