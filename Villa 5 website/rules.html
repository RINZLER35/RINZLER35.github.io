Rules

Villa Rules for Co-Habitation

NOTE: If residents would like to add rules or have any issues, get in touch with me.

  1. If it's not yours, don't use it, simple as that. Ask if you would like to use another residents ingredients, dishware, etc.

  2. Clean all cooking utensils, dishes and areas immediatley or shortly after using them. Do not leave dirty utensils/dishes all day, over night or in your room as others might want to use them or the space to make food.

  3. Be mindful of others peace. Do not make loud noises early in the morning or late at night (i.e. 9pm - 8am).

  4. Lock all doors when you enter and exit the villa for extended periods of time.
