Rules

Villa Rules for Co-Habitation

NOTE: If residents would like to add rules or have any issues, get in touch with me.

  1. If it's not yours, don't use it, simple as that.
    Ask if you would like to use another residents ingredients, dishware, etc.
  2. Clean all cooking utensils, dishes and areas immediatley or shortly after using them.
    Do not leave dirty utensils/dishes all day, over night or in your room as others might want to use them or the space to make food.
  3. Be mindful of others peace.
    Do not make loud noises early in the morning or late at night (i.e. 9pm - 8am).
  4. LOCK ALL DOORS WHEN YOU ENTER AND EXIT THE VILLA FOR EXTENDED PERIODS OF TIME.
    This is a rule to protect everyone and must be followed rigorously and persistenly.
  5. Do not leave washing in the machine or clothes on the line over night.
    This is so other people can access the machine.
  6. Washing machine and washing lines use are a first in first serve basis.
    Unless use is mentioned prior (i.e. the day before) and is not contested.
  7. Full rubbish bags must to be taken to the skip immediatley.
  8. On monthly cleaning weeks, each resident will quickly inspect their own food stores for any off or old food.
    It is not fair to the Kitchen cleaner that they have to handle any rotting or off food that isn't theirs. This also is an opportunity to clear out room of any food that a resident has forgotten or is not intending to eat anytime soon.
