Copyright, Creative Common (CC) Licenses, Fair Use

Basic Copyright Principles
  1. Work: Copyright protects the original expression of an idea rather than the idea itself.
  2. Ownership: The author or creator of a work is the initial owner of the copyright.
  3. Infringement: You cannot do something with that work without the owner's permission.
  4. Exceptions: The Copyright Act has “user rights” that present exceptions to copyright.
  5. Balance: Between the rights of creators, owners, and users.

Web Publishing

3 Factors For Choosing Web Hosting
  1. Server Reliability and Uptime Scores: Low reliability and server uptime causes weak connections, making your website go offline, causing loss in viewership and lowering your SEO ranking.
  2. Customer Service: Going with a provider who has reliable customer support (24/7 support, in-house support department), means that when issues occur, they are resolved more effectively.
  3. Availability Of Site Backups: Having a web hosting service that provides site backups, means that your website and its data is protected from incidents like server/hardware failure, as well as cyber threats.

Web Security

5 Types of Cyber Attacks
  1. Cross-Site Scripting
  2. SQL Injection
  3. Distributed Denial-of-Service attack
  4. Man-in-the-Middle attack
  5. Brute Force Attack
5 Preventitive Measures
  1. Web Application Firewall (Cross-Site Scripting)
  2. Parameterized Statements (SQL Injection)
  3. Content Delivery Network (DDoS Attack)
  4. Secure Sockets Layer (MitM Attack)
  5. 2 Factor Authentication (Brute Force Attack)
