Skate Parks

Bay Skate

Bay Skate is a private park (owned and operated by Napier City Council) based in Napier, with a structure focusing on all styles. The cost of entry is $6 and their hours are: 3pm - 8pm Weekdays and 10am - 8pm Weekends.

William Nelson Park

William Nelson is a public park based in Hastings, focusing primarily on a street/plaza style structure.

Anderson Park

Anderson park is a public park based in Napier. Although it mainly attracts families with a large playground, it has a small skate section that focuses primarily on an street style.
