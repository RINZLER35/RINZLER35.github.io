Getting to Germany - Applying

Applying

Here we will discuss the applying for the exchange.

This is what is included in applying

NOTE: Link additional resources for those considering to learn more before deciding.

Appyling is pretty straight forward, here is the rough process:

  • Inquire with the School of Computing Programme Coordinator (Ian Purdon).
  • He should briefly discuss with you the opportunity then direct you / get you in touch with the international school.
  • Once you have talked with the international school and have confirmed with them your commitment to the exchange, they will nominate you to Furtwangen for exchange.
  • Once you have been nominated, you will have to wait until you hear from Furtwangen and then follow their instructions.

NOTE: add a list of things to do / consider while you wait to hear from your nomination.

Before you commit entirely, make sure you have looked at the following information, so you have a good idea of what is involved and what is required of you.

Instructions for applying to Furtwangen:

Once you have recieved an email from Furtwangen, they will instruct you to apply through their online portal by certain date.
Deadlines for applications:

  • Winter Semester - 1st May
  • Summer Semester - 1st November

To apply go to their website here and click on "Application - you would like to come to HFU for one semester as a guest student" and scroll down to the online portal. Indicate Furtwangen as the campus you want to study at.

The documents you will need to upload fo the application are:

  • Transcript of Records
  • Motivation Letter
  • CV/Resume
  • Proof of language proficiency in German or English
  • Recent Photograph
