Pre-Semester Intensive German Course

Participation in the German Intesive Course (DaF-Intensiv) is mandatory for all students who have not yet achieved B1 level. Students with a level of B1 or above may not attend intensive German courses.

Important information from the School of Languages and Cultures (SLC) regarding your registration for the intensive course:

  • The SLC offers intensive pre-semester German language courses for all new internation students who have not yet achieved B1 proficiency. These courses run at A1 level for beginners with little or no pripr knowledge of German, and at the A2 level for students with an upper-beginner knowledge of German.
  • Registration to the intensive German courses is automatic. SLC will enrol you into the appropriate course based on the information you provided to the International Center.
  • On the last day of the intensive course you will take the German Proficiency Test.
  • If you think that your proficiency level is B1 or above, you will be asked to do a German Placement Test OR upload an official certificate. We accept Telc or Goethe Institue certificates as proof of achievement.
  • When you have been registered by the SLC you will be sent a Welcome Message. Should you not have recieved a message from us by Monday September 11th, please contact us at language.center@hs-furtwangen.de

The Pre-semester Intensive German Courses will run online and start Tuesday, Septemeber 19th. Courses will conclude on Friday, Septemeber, 29th.

For more details on the DaF 1 (Level A1 and A2) Intensive German Course, refer to the orientation programme.

What to bring?
  • Passport or ID-card
  • Completed registration form
  • Wohnungsgeberbescheinigung
    • This form needs to be completed by your landlord, i.e. SWFR (part of your rental documents) or private landlord and handed in as soon as possible.
Important! Before returning to your home country, make sure to de-register at the same office

HFU Email Account

What? Your HFU Email Account will be issued after enrolment
When? We will do an advanced enrolment (before the orientation programme starts) and ask you to send us the necessary documents by Email in advance.
Where? The HFU Email Account will be sent to you by Email.

Enrolment (Einschreibung)

Where? Student Affairs Department, Room a 1.19
What to bring? The International Center will collect all necessary papers and hand them over to the Students Affairs Department.
Each student will have to show appropriate proof of health insurance and the receipt for 66 EUR paid to finalize the enrolment.
Only after having completed the process you will be handed over your HFU card.

HFU Card

What? The HFU-card is the ID card for students and staff based on a multi-functional chip with the following functions: copy card, library card, charge card at the student restaurant, i.e. Mensa dn cafeteria, electronic key (access to HFU buildings outside opening hours), access to the student parking place, for running the washing machine and dryer in GHB.
When? If the advanced enrolment has been successful you will obtain your HFU-card upon arrival with the International Center.
What else? The HFU-card must be activated at the validation machine (A-bulding, left of main entrance).

Residence Permit

What? If your stay in Germany exceeds three months, you need a residence permit (Aufenthaltsgenehmigung)
When? Please send us a copy of your visa as soon as you recieve it. We will check if an appointment with the immigration center will benecessary and help you to prepare all documents needed.
A couple of weeks after registration at the town hall you will be contacted by the Immigration Office and invited to an appointment for a personal visit.
Who? Only NON-EU citizens who have a visa which does not suffice for the whole of their stay.
What to submit?
  • Completed residence permit form
  • Proof of health insurance
  • Biometrical passport photo
  • Financial statement (e.g. parental support or proof of a blocked account)
  • Certificate of enrolment / admission letter
  • Passport
  • Wohnraumbescheinigung (to be filled out by the landlor)
Fees: Period of validity up to one year: 100 Euro
Period of validity more than one year: 100 Euro
Extension up to 3 months: 96 Euro
Extension more than 3 months: 93 Euro

Setting up internet in Grosshasuberg

Direction to the dormitory Grosshausberg:

  • Arrival in Freiburg central train station
  • Take exit "Hauptausgang" and follow the bus sign (ZOB) to the central bus station
  • Take bus no. 7206 direction Waldkirch
  • Change bus in Waldkirch main station to bus no. 7272 direction Furtwangen until station "Ilbenstraße"
  • Turn into Bregstaße for about 200m than turn right into "Baumannstrasse". Turn right into "Bahnhofstrasse" and cross the big street L 173 (Martin-Schmitt-Strasse). Follow straight ahead into "Am Grosshasuberg".
